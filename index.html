About

I'm a product designer and front-end engineer who believes that great software is felt before it's understood. My work lives at the boundary between careful systems thinking and intuitive, human-centered design.

Over eight years, I've shipped products used by millions — from zero-to-one startups to Fortune 500 design systems. I'm drawn to the hard problems: ambiguous briefs, technical constraints, competing stakeholders, and the beautiful tension of making something both functional and delightful.

When I'm not at a screen, you'll find me developing film, cooking elaborate meals, or hiking something with a proper elevation gain.
